What's in a name?

UMBN Collaborative. Say it out loud. What do you hear?

There is a book called CDB. CDB uses letters that sound like words, sometimes called gramograms. When people read the book silently, they typically say they don't understand it. Then, they say it out loud. Try saying the first sentence of the book: "CDB. DBSABZB. OSND." Do you hear it?

See the bee. The bee is a busy bee. Oh, yes indeed.

When Annemarie and Peter first started working together and considered what to call their business, the book sprang to mind. In all of their work, from creative facilitation to digital strategy and thought partnership, collaboration is at the core.
