About UMBN Collaborative

When you say UMBN out loud, what do you hear?

There is a book called CDB. The book begins: “CDB. DBSABZB. OSND.”

(“See the bee. The bee is a busy bee. Oh, yes indeed.”)

It seems like nonsense when read silently. But when people say each letter out loud, they tend to experience a eureka moment as the nonsense transforms into meaning. By participating actively with the book, the reader and book co-create meaning.

At UMBN, we co-create meaningful experiences with our partners. Like the book, our work requires active participation and collaboration with our partners.

UMBN is a collaborative of creatives. It is not a single business. It is a waypoint for our members to showcase what they do. Sometimes, for the right project, we collaborate together, but often we operate individually as freelancers or individual consultants.

Our work

We help teams transform chaos or confusion into clarity, from facilitating workshops to bringing a product or service offering from concept to reality.
